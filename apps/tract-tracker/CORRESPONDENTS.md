# Field Correspondents Guide

## What is a Correspondent?

A **Correspondent** is a person who can take photos during the event and share them to the big screen display. Think of it like a live photo reporter — they capture what's happening in the field and everyone watching the monitor can see it in real time.

---

## For Admins: How to Make Someone a Correspondent

You do this from the **Admin Dashboard**.

**Step-by-step:**

1. Open the app and go to **Admin Dashboard** (only visible if you're an admin).
2. Make sure you're on the **Users** tab — it's the first one and shows the list of all registered people.
3. Find the person you want to make a correspondent.
4. Look to the right side of their row — you'll see a small **camera icon button**.
   - If the camera is just an outline, they are **not yet a correspondent**.
   - Tap it once to turn them into a correspondent. The button turns **gold** to confirm.
   - Tap it again to remove the role.
5. The change takes effect immediately — no need to save.

> **Tip:** A small gold camera badge also appears next to their name so you can quickly spot all correspondents in the list.

---

## For Correspondents: How to Upload a Photo

Once an admin has given you the Correspondent role, a new **Camera tab** will appear in your app's bottom navigation bar.

**Here's how to post a photo:**

1. Open the app and tap the **Camera tab** at the bottom (it looks like a camera icon).
2. You'll see your **Field Reports** screen. This is where your uploaded photos will appear.
3. To take a new photo, tap the **gold camera button** at the bottom right of the screen.
   - You can also tap the **camera icon at the top right** to take a photo.
   - Or tap the **images icon at the top right** to choose an existing photo from your gallery.
4. Your phone's camera (or photo library) will open. Take or select your photo.
5. A preview window will appear showing your photo.
   - You can type a **caption** to describe what's happening (optional, max 280 characters).
6. Tap the **"Post Report"** button to upload it.
   - Wait a moment while it uploads (you'll see a loading spinner on the button).
7. Once done, your photo will appear in your Field Reports list and on the **News Feed screen** for everyone watching the monitor to see.

---

## The News Feed Screen (For Monitors and Big Screens)

This screen is designed to be shown on a **projector or big monitor** during the event. It displays all the field photos from correspondents in a live slideshow.

**How to open it on a monitor:**

1. Open a web browser (Chrome, Edge, etc.) on the monitor or laptop connected to the screen.
2. Go to this address: **`https://tracttracker.app/news-feed`**
3. Press **F11** on the keyboard to go full screen.
4. That's it! The screen will update automatically every 30 seconds. No need to touch anything.

**What you'll see:**

- A large featured photo at the top that **automatically cycles** through the latest photos every 8 seconds.
- A grid of all uploaded photos below it.
- A **green "NEW" badge** on photos that were uploaded within the last 5 minutes.
- The name of the reporter and the time they posted.
- The live count of how many photos have been posted.

> **Note:** Anyone can view the News Feed — you don't need to be logged in.

## Common Questions

**Q: I don't see the Camera tab in my app.**
A: Ask your admin to mark you as a correspondent. Once they tap the camera button next to your name in the Users list, close and reopen the app.

**Q: My photo isn't showing on the big screen.**
A: The screen updates every 30 seconds. Wait a moment and it should appear. If it still doesn't show, make sure you tapped "Post Report" and saw the upload complete.

**Q: Can I post more than one photo?**
A: Yes! You can post as many photos as you want throughout the event.

**Q: Do I need internet to take photos?**
A: You need internet to upload. Take the photo first, but make sure you're connected before tapping "Post Report."
